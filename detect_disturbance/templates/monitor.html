Record Animal Entry & Exit

It has been theorized that animals get very restless, when they sense an impending earthquake, and hence the idea was to record the entry and exit of animals in local zoos, and use the anomaly in the entry and exit patterns in case of animals, to issue a sign of warning of earthquakes.
